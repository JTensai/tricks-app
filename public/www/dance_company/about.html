Purpose: The Tricks Dance Company philosophy is; “Family first, then school, then dance. We believe in quality time in the dance studio not quantity. Our goal with this company is to challenge each student to develop their own strength, flexibility, poise and stage presence. This will in turn build strong self-confidence and hopefully a strong love for the art of dance.”

Dance Company is for those students who love to perform! When in company, the focus is on learning choreography, and performing outside of Tricks in the community. Our dancers perform all over Placer, El dorado and Sacramento County. In our 2014 season we hope to perform at Disneyland in April! Company requires a large commitment from dancers as well as their families. Company dancers are required to attend multiple Saturday rehearsals in addition to their regular company class.
